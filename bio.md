#

Einar Ingebrigtsen is a Norwegian software developer/architect and works as a technical advisor at Novanet.
He is the creator of Cratis, an open-source event sourcing, CQRS and productivity platform for .NET.
His passion lies in building quality software that improves the lives of end users, and his focus has been on event sourcing and event-driven architecture since 2010.

His background spans games development on various platforms, telecom, broadcast TV, and a wide range of line-of-business systems.
Along the way, he has also been a Silverlight MVP (later Client MVP when Silverlight mysteriously disappeared) but had to stop being an MVP in favor of
working at Microsoft as a technical evangelist at one point.

He really enjoys helping other developers with the building blocks they need to create maintainable software.
